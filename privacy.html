Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Unavailable Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website and use our Roblox games or Discord server features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Website Data: We do not collect personal data directly through our website. However, third-party services like GitHub Pages or your browser may collect anonymized data such as IP address or device info.
  • Roblox Game Data: Within Roblox games, we may collect your username, user ID, and gameplay statistics to provide features such as leaderboards, rewards, or moderation. This is limited to in-game only.
  • Discord Data: If you connect with our Discord bot or server, your Discord user ID, messages, and roles may be stored for bot features, moderation, or role synchronization. This is in line with Discord’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate game features (e.g., progress tracking, leaderboard stats).
  • To enhance safety and enforce rules (e.g., moderation or bans).
  • To sync roles or activity from Discord with Roblox.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law. Some features rely on third-party platforms:

  • Discord Terms of Service
  • Roblox Terms of Use
  • Roblox Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy & Age Requirements

Roblox may be used by children under 13, but our Discord integrations are intended for users age 13 and up. If you're under 13, you may not join our Discord server in compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service.

Note: Minimum age requirements may vary by country. For details, refer to Discord’s Safety Center and Roblox Help Center.

5. Cookies

This website may use cookies through GitHub Pages or your browser for anonymous analytics. We do not set or collect cookies ourselves.

6. Monetization & Advertising

Some Roblox games may include monetized items (e.g., game passes, developer products) which are subject to Roblox's payment system. We do not collect any billing or payment data directly.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data through the platforms we use (e.g., Discord, Roblox). We do not store passwords, emails, or payment info.

8. Your Rights

You may request that we remove any stored data (such as moderation logs) by contacting us via Discord or Roblox. For platform data, see:

  • Discord Privacy Policy
  • Roblox Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. It is your responsibility to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

For any privacy concerns or questions, contact us via our Discord server or on our Roblox group Unavalible Games.
