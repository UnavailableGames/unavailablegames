Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Unavailable Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you access our website, play our Roblox games, or interact with our Discord server and bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • Website: We do not actively collect personal information on this website. GitHub Pages and your browser may collect anonymized technical data such as IP address and device type for analytics purposes.
  • Roblox: When using our Roblox games, we may collect in-game identifiers like username, user ID, and game progress to enable features such as leaderboards and moderation.
  • Discord: If you join our Discord server or use our bot, we may process your Discord user ID, messages, roles, and server activity. This is used solely for moderation, community features, and Discord-Roblox synchronization, and is subject to the Discord Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use the Information

Information is used for the following purposes only:

  • Operating and improving game and bot features (e.g., data tracking, role syncing).
  • Ensuring user safety and enforcing community rules.
  • Responding to abuse reports and moderating content.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent any data. However, we may share data under the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or platform terms.
  • When using integrated third-party services such as:
    • Discord
    • Roblox

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Roblox games may be used by children under 13 in accordance with Roblox’s policies. However, participation in our Discord server and usage of our Discord bot is restricted to users 13 years and older per the Discord Terms of Service.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our site does not set cookies directly. GitHub Pages or your browser may use cookies for performance analytics. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

6. Payments and Monetization

Our Roblox games may include monetized features like developer products or game passes, all of which are handled by Roblox. We do not process or store any payment or billing information.

7. Data Security

We take steps to ensure the security of your information by relying on platform-level protections (e.g., Discord and Roblox security measures). We do not store passwords, payment information, or contact details.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of data we control, such as moderation logs or Discord role sync data. To do so, contact us via our official channels listed below. For platform-managed data (e.g., Roblox account details), please refer directly to:

  • Discord Privacy Policy
  • Roblox Privacy Policy

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the revised terms.

10. Contact

If you have questions or privacy concerns, please reach out to us via:

  • Our Discord Server
  • Our Roblox Group
